Le Petit George is a charming little neighbourhood bistro created by George, located just a few steps away from the famously bustling George W.P.A. LPG pays homage to the beloved French bistro, where good food, great wine and even better company come together to create an unmistakable joie de vivre. ‍ The indoor-outdoor space is beautifully decorated with lush plants, mirrors, signature subway tiles and marble surfaces. ssThonet chairs and handwritten menu boards showcasing daily specials round out this airy space. Whether you are relaxing in the glasshouse, pulling up a seat at a cosy bar corner, or dining al fresco — this is a warm and unpretentious place where neighbors gather to share stories over a good meal. ‍ In keeping with George’s tradition, LPG is open throughout the day, and the food is limited to a small, curated selection of French traditional favorites and George classics. ‍ One can enjoy a cafe au lait and a freshly baked croissant at breakfast, a quick healthy salad for lunch, and a board of charcuterie and fromage alongside chilled whites or bold reds from mid-afternoon onwards. Or come for a killer beer, and stay for an early dinner or late supper with friends and family.
